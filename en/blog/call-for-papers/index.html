CALL FOR PAPERS

Posted: Sep 1, 2016
Who we are looking for?

We are looking for talented and uninhibited developers/QA/etc. It’s time to admit to yourself - you’re waiting for a moment of glory… Come and take!

We are interested in the hardcore technical presentations, hands-on sessions, researches. We offer: 400+ conferees and travel-support (if you are not in N.Novgorod, or even not in Russia).

We have prepared topics which are snazzy in our humble opinion. But if you have your own five-star idea – feel free to offer us a topic not out of the list below.

Topics
  • Android
  • Internet of Things
  • Google Cloud & Google APIs
  • Virtual and Augmented Reality
  • Modern Web Application Development
  • Firebase
  • etc.
Please use this form to submit your proposal.
Deadline: 1th of November 2016.

The program committee picks the most interesting talk proposals and informs the selected submitters until November 15, 2016.
